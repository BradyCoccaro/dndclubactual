About The Club Members

Cooper is the head of our club, having started it up during the 2021-2022 school year. Cooper manages meetings and gives club announcements, and is currently a player in a campaign DMed by Garrett Middleton.

Name: Will Lewensten

Title: Dungeon master

Year: Freshman

Will acts as our "Tutorial DM", alongside Joshua, who keeps things to a basic level and explains rules to new players. Will teaches the new players and runs an enjoyable campaign for his group.

Name: Joshua Buenavides

Title: Dungeon Master

Year: Sophomore

As well as DMing Josh runs our "One Shot Pot" which is where anyone who brings community food will donate food or drinks which are shared with the club. Joshua monitors food to make sure people don't greedily take too much.
